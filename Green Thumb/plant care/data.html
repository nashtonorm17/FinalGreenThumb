Plant Care Information

Azaela

  • Scientific Name: Rhododendron
  • Color: pink, red, yellow, purple, white
  • Watering: 1 gallon weekly (per bush)
  • Soil: aerated, loamy, well-drained soil
  • Sunlight: 4-6 hours of partial/shaded of light daily

Basil
  • Scientific Name: Ocimum basilicum
  • Color: green
  • Watering: 1 inch weekly
  • Soil: slightly acidic, well-drained soil
  • Sunlight: 10-12 hours of direct light daily
Begonia
  • Scientific Name: Begonia obliqua
  • Color: pink, orange, yellow, white
  • Watering: 1/2 - 1 every 3-4 days
  • Soil: moist, well-drained soil
  • Sunlight: 4-6 hours of indirect sunlight
Cabbage
  • Scientific Name: Brassica oleracea

  • Soil: loamy, well-drained soil
  • Sunlight: 6-8 hours of direct light daily
Carnation

  • Watering: 1-2 inches weekly
  • Soil: alkaline, well-drained soil
  • Sunlight: 6-8 hours direct/partial light daily
